Spaghetti Cacio e Pepe

Spaghetti Cacio e Pepe Recipe

This is a very simple recipe on how to cook this amazing italian dish in only 25 minutes, delicious and easy to make when you dont have to go to the shop.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 3/4 cups grated Pecorino Romano cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large port of salted water to a boil and then cook the spaghetti.
  2. Add the oil to a pan on medium heat and add garlic and pepper
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti and Pecorino
  4. Add some on the reserved cooking water
  5. Stir until the cheese is melted
