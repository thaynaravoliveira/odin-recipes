Breaded Chicken Recipe

This is a very simple recipe on how to cook a Breaded Chicken in only 35 minutes, it server 4 people, it is a great option for a weekday dinner for a family.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of bread crumbs
  • 1(1ounce) package dry French onion soup mix
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste (Optional)
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C)
  2. Mix bread crumbs and dry soup mix together in a bowl
  3. Mix mayonnaise and garlic paste in a separate bowl for 20 seconds
  4. Brush the chicken with the mayonnaise mix
  5. Cover the chicken with the breadcrumbs and place it on a baking sheet
  6. Bake for 20 minutes.
